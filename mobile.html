Commercial cleaning questions

Useful answers for office managers, property managers, clinic owners, and operations teams comparing cleaning companies.

How much does commercial cleaning cost?

Pricing depends on square footage, cleaning frequency, washroom count, floor type, traffic, supplies, and whether you need day porter, floor care, disinfection, or post-construction cleaning. A walk-through produces the most accurate quote.

Do you offer office cleaning after business hours?

Yes. Cleaning can be scheduled during the day, after hours, overnight, or on weekends so staff and customers are not interrupted.

What types of businesses need recurring janitorial service?

Professional offices, medical clinics, dental offices, retail stores, warehouses, condominiums, schools, restaurants, dealerships, and multi-tenant properties often use recurring janitorial contracts.

Can you build a custom cleaning checklist?

Yes. Each contract can include a site-specific checklist for reception areas, workstations, boardrooms, kitchens, washrooms, floors, carpets, garbage removal, recycling, glass, vents, and high-touch surfaces.
